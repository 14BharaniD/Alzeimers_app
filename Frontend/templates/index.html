Multimodal Framework for Early Detection of Alzheimer's Disease

Our advanced deep learning system analyzes handwriting patterns and speech characteristics to identify early signs of Alzheimer's disease, enabling timely intervention and better patient outcomes.

Our Advanced Detection Features

Handwriting Analysis

Extracts 450+ numerical features from handwriting samples including pressure, stroke velocity, tremors, and spatial organization to detect micrographia and other early signs.

Speech Recognition

Analyzes MFCC (Mel-frequency cepstral coefficients) from voice recordings to identify speech patterns, hesitation, word-finding difficulties, and phonetic changes associated with cognitive decline.

Deep Learning Integration

Combines both modalities using convolutional and recurrent neural networks to achieve higher predictive accuracy than single-modality approaches.

How Our System Works
